We scan buildings and digitize portfolios.

  1. We do our Research. First, we learn about your space: how many floors, how it's structured, we review model layout documents, we assemble an idea for how much work will need to go into this to make things work well.
  2. We scan your Reality. Then, once the plan has been cleared and the expectations are properly set, we scan. Using state-of-the-art sensor equipment, we capture your reality and generate point clouds and create the digital twins.
  3. We model the Digital Twins. Our modeling team (exclusively Architects and Engineers) process the data we gather and model a twin of the scanned information. We have an extensive QA/QC workflow that ensures near perfection.
  4. We host your Digital Twins. Finally: Your buildings, online and in the cloud. Forget the nuanced self-management and organization of this information. It's confusing - we get it.
